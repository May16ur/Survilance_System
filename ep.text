what is the issue?
Current classification rules are mostly plate-based:

Military Vehicle

Plate starts with broad-arrow style marker if detected.
Plate pattern looks like military format, for example year/code/serial style:
24B141317E

1 19B131092M
first take only 2 digit before Alphabet


2D189806Y
second always end whith the alphabet

Plate/background hints show military colors:
black / olive background
white or light text
Vehicle metadata or type contains military/defence hints.
Civil Vehicle

Indian RTO-style plate pattern:
JK 10A3931
LA 02A7555
MH 01EJ8131
DL 8CAD1682
State/UT prefix is recognized:
JK, LA, MH, DL, HR, etc.
Normal private/commercial plate hints:
white/yellow/green plate background with black text.
Unknown Vehicle

Plate is empty, UNKNOWN, too short, or low confidence.
Plate format does not safely match military or civil.
Camera sends vehicle image but no plate number.
OCR text looks noisy or incomplete.
Important: this classification does not depend on YOLO now. It uses CP Plus ANPR fields: plate number, plate color/type, vehicle type, and metadata.
